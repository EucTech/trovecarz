Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a team of car enthusiasts and tech experts, Trovecarz was born out of frustration with the traditional car-buying experience in Nigeria.

We saw too many people being misled, overcharged, or buying faulty vehicles. So we decided to change that — forever.

Today, Trovecarz is the fastest-growing used car marketplace in Nigeria, connecting thousands of verified buyers and sellers every month.
